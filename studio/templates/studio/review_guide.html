Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I edit my review after submitting it?

A: Yes! You can update your review by visiting the same game page and clicking the review button again.

Q: Can I review the same game multiple times?

A: You can have one active review per game, but you can update it anytime as your opinion evolves.

Q: Do I earn experience for writing reviews?

A: Yes! Writing reviews and participating in the community earns you experience points and achievements.

Q: What other community features are available?

A: You can also join game forums, upload screenshots/videos, and rate games with detailed category breakdowns!
